Deal Summary

Kingswood Cove Apartments is a 312-unit multifamily value-add acquisition in Round Rock, TX (Austin-Round Rock MSA) acquired at $30M ($96,154/unit) on a 7.45% going-in cap rate. The property is 93.2% occupied with an active renovation program (87/312 units complete) generating a $121/month rent premium. With 65% LTV fixed-rate financing at 6.25%, the deal projects a 14.8% 5-year IRR and 1.73x MOIC, supported by strong Austin MSA fundamentals including 3.2% population growth and 5.1% trailing rent growth. DSCR of 1.50x provides a 25-basis-point buffer above the lender minimum.
